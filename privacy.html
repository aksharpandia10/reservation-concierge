Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Reservation Concierge ("we," "us") handles information when you use our SMS text-messaging service.

Information we collect

When you text us, we collect your mobile phone number and the content of the messages you send (for example, your party size, neighborhood, preferred date and time, and cuisine preferences). We do not ask for or collect payment information, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

How we use it

  • To understand your request and reply with restaurants that have availability.
  • To send you booking links for restaurants you choose.
  • To maintain the context of your conversation so we can respond helpfully.

How we share it

To find availability, we send only your reservation details — such as neighborhood, date, time, and party size — to reservation platforms (for example, Resy and OpenTable) and to our AI provider to generate replies. We use these solely to fulfill your request. We do not share your mobile phone number with these platforms.

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share, sell, or otherwise provide your mobile phone number or messaging consent information to any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third parties or affiliates.

SMS consent & opt-out

You opt in to receive text messages from us by texting our number first — your message is your consent to receive our replies. We never message anyone who has not messaged us first. You can text STOP at any time to opt out and HELP for help. Message frequency varies; message and data rates may apply. Opting out stops all further messages.

Data retention

We retain conversation history only as long as needed to provide the service, and you may request deletion of your data by contacting us.
