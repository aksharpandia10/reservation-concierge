Reservation Concierge

text us, and we'll find you a table in nyc.

What it is

Reservation Concierge is a text-message assistant that helps you find open restaurant reservations in New York City. Tell us your party size, neighborhood, and when you'd like to dine, and we'll reply with restaurants that have availability and a link to book.

How to use it

Text us at +1 (914) 274-4936 with something like "2 for dinner in the West Village Friday around 8." You'll get a few options with open times and booking links. We hand you the link — you complete the reservation on the restaurant's own page. We do not book on your behalf or take payment.

How consent works (opt-in)

This is a two-way, conversational service. You opt in by texting our number first with a reservation request — sending us that first message is your express consent to receive reply messages from Reservation Concierge about your request. We never send messages to anyone who has not messaged us first, and there is no automatic or recurring marketing.

When you first text us, you'll receive a confirmation: "Reservation Concierge: you're in! … Reply HELP for help, STOP to opt out. Msg & data rates may apply; msg frequency varies." You can text STOP at any time to opt out and HELP for help. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

No mobile information — including your phone number, SMS opt-in data, and messaging consent — will be shared with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
